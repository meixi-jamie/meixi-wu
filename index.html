A Spring

Twelve frames from one slow afternoon. Pink blossoms, city sweets, grass under late sun, and a hillside turning gold.

Prologue

Some afternoons are worth stretching into forever.

This is a small collection of moments from a single spring — the kind that begins with flowers overhead and ends with your shoes dusty from a hillside.

I'm Meixi. I like images that don't try too hard: soft light, real color, and the feeling that the person in the frame was somewhere they actually wanted to be.
